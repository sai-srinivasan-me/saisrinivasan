What is a portfolio?

An ePortfolio (electronic portfolio) is a multimedia compilation of work created over time to demonstrate talents,beliefs, interests, and competencies using a variety of evidence-based learning methods. An ePortfolio may contain a variety of items, such as relevant documents and media files, that give a comprehensive picture of who you are personally, professionally, and academically.
